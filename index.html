About FieldEnable

FieldEnable is an Android app which tracks the Field employee activity, who is always on the Go. FieldEnable app covers all the activities that are a part and parcel of Field Service Management. The Salesteam / Field Staff can meet clients, present product based presentataions, Submit product samples, update Instant feedback and Book orders from their prospective clients. Check-in and Check-out along with Visit result and Duration of Meetings/visits will be synced from time to time. FieldEnable records daily visit efforts and calculates the distance travelled over the period of the day. The app is also supported by an admin panel to track the locations of Field Staff and manage their day to day efforts by capturing authentic data from their visited locations. End to end Tracking of the Field Staff from the Start of Shift to End of Shift will improve productivity of field Team.
